# AI Log

## Purpose
This folder is the Assignment 1 AI usage log.
It records how AI assistance was used during analysis, what outputs were kept, what was rejected or narrowed, and how final claims were validated against the APK evidence.

## How This Folder Meets the Spec / Rubric
This folder is designed to satisfy the explicit AI-related expectations summarized in the project spec and rubric:
- AI usage is submitted as a separate component rather than being mixed only into the report
- at least one step-by-step AI interaction is recorded in summary form
- the retained workflow shows where AI helped with tooling, search strategy, wording, and rehearsal
- the log explains how AI-assisted outputs were manually checked against decompiled code, screenshots, and assignment constraints
- at least one rubric-driven LLM mock Q&A rehearsal is documented

## Included Files
- `workflow-summary.md`
  - high-level summary of where AI was used across the project workflow
- `prompt-response-summaries.md`
  - compact summaries of representative AI interactions and outputs
- `validation-record.md`
  - how the team checked AI-assisted outputs against decompiled code, screenshots, and rubric constraints
- `mock-qa-log.md`
  - rubric-driven LLM rehearsal summary and resulting wording changes
- `tools-and-docs-references.md`
  - references for AI tools, analysis tools, and assignment documents used during the workflow

## Boundary Statement
AI was used to support workflow setup, organize evidence, draft bounded wording, and rehearse tutorial Q&A.
The final vulnerability claim, attacker model, attack path, mitigation wording, and evidence citations were checked manually against the decompiled APK and project evidence before being retained.

## Important Constraint
This AI log is a summary log, not a raw export of every chat turn.
It is intended to satisfy the assignment requirement to document AI-assisted workflow and validation.
